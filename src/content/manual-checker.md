# KTC Online Portal — X-ray Checker Guide

Your station is the **X-ray Checker** queue — you land there at sign-in. One job: confirm containers through the X-ray line and answer "is this box cleared?"

---

## 1 · Signing in

- Use your **username** and password on the portal login page (no email needed).
- The queue shows every order waiting for X-ray, **sorted by line number**, with the **Now serving** strip on top.

## 2 · Looking up a container

Type a container number into the lookup:

- **NOT CLEARED · X-ray pending** — still waiting for the X-ray.
- **CLEARED** with date and time — it already passed.

Use this whenever a trucker or broker asks about a specific box.

## 3 · Confirming X-ray done

When a container passes the X-ray, hit **Confirm**:

- The date and time are stamped permanently (the lookup will show them).
- The order leaves your queue. If X-ray was its last pending service it completes; if it still needs DEA or OOG it stays open for those — either way it's out of your line.

## 4 · The weekly line

Serving numbers reset every Monday; open orders from last week are re-queued **at the front, in their old order** — so the top of your Monday queue is last week's unfinished business, then the new week's filings.

## 5 · Your session

- The tablet **signs out after 60 idle minutes**. An "Are you still there?" prompt appears one minute before — one tap keeps it alive.
- **One active session per account:** if the same login is used on another device, this tablet is signed out with a notice. If that happens unexpectedly, tell the admin.
- Never share your password; the admin can reset it anytime.

---

*Anything beyond confirm-and-lookup (holds, rejects, payments) belongs to the admin or cashier — flag it rather than working around it.*
