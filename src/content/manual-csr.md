# KTC Online Portal — CSR Guide

For **Customer Service Representatives**. You are the customer's first point of contact: you answer support tickets, review the consignees customers ask KTC to add, verify pull-out documents, and help file orders. (You don't take payments or process the X-ray line — flag those to cashier / operations.)

---

## 1 · Signing in

- Use your **username** and password on the login page (no email needed).
- You land on the **Help & Support** inbox — the customer messages waiting for a reply.

## 2 · Help & Support (your main job)

- Each ticket shows the customer, the category, and the conversation. **Reply** in-app and the customer is notified; the ticket becomes **Answered**.
- A ticket is **Open** (waiting on KTC), **Answered** (you replied), or **Closed** (resolved — closing locks the thread).
- **Open a ticket on a customer's behalf** for a phone / walk-in / off-platform enquiry, so every contact is logged in one place.

## 3 · Consignee requests

When a customer asks KTC to add a new consignee, it lands in **Consignees** for review:

- Open the request, check the details (trade name, address, TIN, BIR 2303), then **Approve**, ask for more with **Needs info** (the customer edits and resubmits), or **Reject** (with a reason — the customer can adjust and request again).
- Once **approved**, the consignee appears in the customer's filing picker.

## 4 · Filing a job order (intake)

- You can file a **job order on a customer's behalf** (walk-in / phone intake) from **New JO** — it goes straight to the queue with a serving number and a printable slip.
- You can also **request priority** on an order to move it ahead of the X-ray line (an admin approves it).

## 5 · Release / Pull-out documents

- For container pull-out requests you can **verify the DO / BL documents** (the documents-desk step), moving a release from **Submitted** to **Documents verified** so the charge can be set. Setting charges, confirming payment, and recording the OR are the cashier's / documents-desk's steps.

## 6 · Your session

- Signs out after **60 idle minutes** — an "Are you still there?" prompt appears a minute before; one tap keeps it alive.
- **One active session per account** — signing in elsewhere signs this one out.
- Never share your password; the admin can reset it anytime.

---

*Anything outside intake + comms (payments, the X-ray line, configuration) belongs to cashier / operations / admin — flag it rather than working around it.*
